Stage 1 · Empathise

When small mistakes
become big accidents.

Every year, preventable kitchen fires and gas explosions claim lives. The common cause: human forgetfulness under stress.

👵

The Elderly User

An elderly person forgets to turn off the stove after cooking. The unattended flame ignites nearby materials, escalating into a dangerous fire — unnoticed until it's too late.

👨‍👩‍👧

The Busy Parent

A parent steps away to attend to children while cooking. The stove runs unattended for extended periods, resulting in overheating, burnt food, and serious fire risk.

🌙

The Night Gas Leak

A faulty stove knob leads to an invisible gas leak overnight. With no auto-shutoff system, LPG silently accumulates — creating a life-threatening explosion risk by morning.

Stage 4 · Prototype · Working Model

Detect. Decide. Act.
In under 3 seconds.

A five-stage intelligence loop executes entirely autonomously — even while you sleep.

01
Monitor

Continuous Sensing

The MQ-2 gas sensor and LM35 temperature sensor continuously sample the environment around your stove — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, without interruption.

02
Process

Real-Time Logic

Arduino Nano firmware reads sensor values and runs the decision algorithm in real-time. Threshold logic: gas above 400 ppm, or temperature above 80°C triggers the next stage.

03
Assess

Hazard Classification

The system classifies the situation as safe or hazardous based on multi-sensor data. A single sensor spike does not trigger action — the system is designed to avoid false positives.

04
Alert

Immediate Warning

The buzzer activates immediately. Simultaneously, the Bluetooth module sends a push notification to the user's smartphone — wherever they are in the home.

05
Shutoff

Physical Shutdown

The SG90 servo motor physically rotates the stove knob to the OFF position. This is mechanical enforcement — the gas supply is cut regardless of any other condition.

Stage 4 · Hardware

Six components.
One complete system.

Carefully selected off-the-shelf components working in unison to form a fully autonomous safety layer.

🖥️

Arduino Nano

Central Processing Unit

The brain of the system — reads all sensor inputs, runs the decision firmware, and controls every output component.

💨

MQ-2 Gas Sensor

Gas Detection

Detects LPG, propane, and methane gas. Triggers the safety sequence when concentration exceeds 400 ppm.

🌡️

LM35 Temperature Sensor

Heat Monitoring

Precision analog sensor that detects abnormal heat buildup at the stove surface — triggers at 80°C.

⚙️

SG90 Servo Motor

Knob Actuator

Physically rotates the stove knob to OFF position when danger is detected. Mechanical enforcement — no bypass.

📡

HC-05 Bluetooth Module

Wireless Communication

Enables real-time two-way communication with the companion mobile app for alerts and manual control.

🔔

Buzzer

Local Alert System

Emits an immediate audible alarm when a hazard is detected — alerts anyone in or near the kitchen instantly.
